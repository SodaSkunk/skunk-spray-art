Welcome!

Hi! My name is Soda (he/they). I am an illustration artist, sculptor and costume maker currently residing in Antwerp. My work mainly centers around animals, folklore creatures, masks and the queer body.

My main activities at the moment are vending at art markets and creating illustrations for organisations and individuals. Besides that I am planning to expand my activities around costume creation.
